The Story

A Restoobar Unlike Any Other

Rooted in the authentic heritage of Kannada culture, Sarovara Restoobar blends traditional Naati-style culinary secrets with a premium fine dining atmosphere. Located next to Kengeri Metro Station, Mysore Road, we celebrate generous portions, hand-crafted cocktails, and a soulful Kannada musical ambiance.
