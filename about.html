Our Story & Heritage

Crafting authentic culinary masterpieces in the heart of Kengeri since day one.

A Legendary Heritage

Rooted In Tradition

Established as a landmark destination in Kengeri, Bengaluru, Sarovara Restoobar was born out of a deep-seated passion for the authentic, traditional culinary legacy of Karnataka. Our mission is simple: to offer a fine dining experience that respects the rich agricultural and culinary roots of our state while delivering a premium, modern ambiance.

Our kitchen takes direct inspiration from the rustic kitchens of rural Karnataka—celebrating the spicy, robust, and flavorful Naati-style cuisine. We source all our local spices directly from regional markets, and our chefs hand-grind every masala daily. From the legendary Naati Mutton Biryani to our spicy mutton chops and Karavali seafood specialties, every dish is a culinary journey.

But Sarovara Restoobar is not just about the food. It is a sensory immersion. As you enjoy your meal, you are surrounded by the gentle warmth of our gold-accented, low-lit seating and the soulful sounds of live Kannada music performances. Here, the values of traditional hospitality meet state-of-the-art dining, making us Kengeri's premier destination for families and friends.
