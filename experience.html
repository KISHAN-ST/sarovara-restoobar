Live Music Nights

We believe music connects souls. Every evening, our stage lights up with renowned local artists performing acoustic sets of evergreen Kannada classics and contemporary hits, providing a soulful and nostalgic soundtrack to your dining.

Host your corporate meets, birthday celebrations, or family get-togethers in our custom soundproofed private dining rooms. Equipped with comfortable premium seating, ambient lights, and dedicated service hosts.

Our bar celebrates local fruits and spices in mixology. Enjoy our famous happy hour, showcasing single malts, draft beers, and custom cocktails paired with signature hot pepper-fry and roasted mutton naati snacks.
